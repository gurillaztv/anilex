Good to know

Collecting with a clear conscience.

Your photos stay your photos.

Your shots and your whole collection live on your device. For the species recognition alone, a downscaled copy of the photo goes to our recognition service and on to Google Gemini – without your name, your location or any identifier, and it is not stored there permanently.

  • No account, no registration, no login
  • Originals, stickers and your catch map stay on your device
  • Details in the Privacy Policy

With respect for nature.

Anilex is about watching, not touching: on launch the app reminds you of the most important conservation rules – so that every catch is a good moment for the animal, too.

  • Keep your distance, never lure or feed
  • Leave nests and young animals in peace
  • Stay on the paths, respect protected areas
